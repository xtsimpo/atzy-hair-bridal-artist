The bridal package includes a pre-wedding trial session, a rehearsal appointment, the wedding day bridal hairstyle, and carefully selected hair accessories chosen exclusively to complement your look. Hair extensions are also available upon request and prior arrangement.

We are available to travel throughout Greece, covering all regions and destinations. Service availability may vary depending on the date and location, so we recommend reaching out early to confirm your wedding day arrangements.

Yes, additional hairstyles for bridesmaids, mothers of the bride or groom, and other guests can be arranged on the wedding day. Availability depends on the overall schedule and the total number of people requiring styling, so early coordination is highly recommended.

The trial session can take place at the bride's home or at any other location that is most convenient for her. We are happy to accommodate your preferences to ensure a relaxed and comfortable experience.

While makeup is not offered directly, we work closely with a network of trusted professional makeup artists whom we can recommend and coordinate with, ensuring a seamless and cohesive bridal look from start to finish.

Yes, pre-wedding and next-day hairstyling services are available upon request and subject to availability. Please note that these are offered as additional services and are priced separately from the main bridal hair package.

During the trial session, we explore multiple variations of your desired hairstyle, taking into account your preferences, outfit, and overall bridal aesthetic. This allows you to make a confident and informed decision before your wedding day.
